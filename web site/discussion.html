Discussion Forum

While there is no official support for the Strongtalk system, there is a Google discussion group that can be used as a forum for questions and comments. At least one of us plans on regularly responding to postings on the discussion group as necessary. The group is:

strongtalk-general

Come on over and share your thoughts! If you are interested in Strongtalk, we would be more than happy to try to help you along.

We are just starting to organize Strongtalk as an open source project, since the VM source has just been released. If you are a VM or Smalltalk wizard, and are interested in helping out, now is the time to get in!
